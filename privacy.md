 # Privacy Policy for Dull Blobs                            
                                                             
  Last updated: 2026-05-29                                   
                                                             
  This privacy policy describes how Dull Blobs ("the app", "we") handles your information when you use our mobile app.              
                                                           
  ## What we collect                                       

  **Purchase information.** When you make an in-app purchase, Apple processes the transaction. We use RevenueCat to manage entitlements (which packs and lives you own). RevenueCat receives an anonymized user ID and purchase records. We do not receive your name, email, billing address, or credit card details.                 
                                                             
  **Local app data.** Your game progress, settings, coin count, and unlocked packs are stored on your device using iOS's AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you uninstall the app (in which case it is deleted).                           
                                                           
  **Bug reports.** If you submit a bug report through the in-app form, your report is sent to Google Forms. We use the report to fix issues. We do not collect your contact information unless you voluntarily include it.
                                                             
  **Crash and performance data.** Apple and Expo (our development platform) may collect anonymous crash logs and performance data to help us improve app stability. This data does not identify you.                                                       
                                                           
  ## What we don't do                                      

  - We don't use third-party advertising networks.           
  - We don't sell your data.
  - We don't track you across other apps or websites.        
  - We don't collect data from children under 13 beyond what is described above. This app does not knowingly request personal information from any user.                             
                                                             
  ## Your rights                                           
                                                             
  You can delete all locally-stored data by deleting the app from your device. To restore previous in-app purchases on a new device, sign in with the same Apple ID and use the "Restore Purchases" option in the app's settings.
